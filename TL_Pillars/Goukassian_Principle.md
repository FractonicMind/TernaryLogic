## THE OPERATIONAL TRINITY

### The Lantern (Artifact of Conscience)

**Definition**: The cryptographically sealed record of the Epistemic Hold (the '0' state)

**Function**: Immutable proof that contextual deliberation occurred before action

**Content Requirements**:
- Rationale for entering Epistemic Hold
- Alternatives considered during pause
- Risk assessments performed
- Rules and thresholds evaluated
- Confidence calculations
- Decision justification

**Technical Implementation**: 
- Generated during every '0' state activation
- Captured in Parallel Ledger Lane
- Cryptographically hashed before sealing
- Anchored to heterogeneous blockchains

### The Signature (Cryptographic Validity)

**Definition**: Digital attestation of Lantern legitimacy and integrity

**Function**: Mathematical guarantee of authentic deliberation record

**Process**:
1. Generated by certified TL Core Module
2. Anchored to multiple public blockchains
3. Creates the "Inviolable Trace"
4. Fulfills "Evidence Must Catch Up" axiom

**Verification**: Any party can independently verify signature validity against public anchors

### The License (Conditional Authority)

**Definition**: Dynamic mandate to operate under Ternary Logic framework

**Enforcement Mechanism**:
- Continuously contingent on Lantern generation
- Monitored by governance bodies
- Revoked immediately upon:
  - Failure to generate valid Lantern
  - Unsigned or tampered Lantern
  - Violation of core prohibitions
  - Bypass of Epistemic Hold

**Cardinal Rule**: **Forfeit the Lantern, Forfeit the License**
