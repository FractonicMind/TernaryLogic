**Subverting accountability mechanisms:**

- ESG greenwashing (violating Pillar 6: Sustainable Capital)

**Privacy violations:**
- Misusing Hybrid Shield (Pillar 7) to expose protected data

- Selling decision data without authorization

**Note:** No monetary damages sought - enforcement focuses on protecting TL integrity.
