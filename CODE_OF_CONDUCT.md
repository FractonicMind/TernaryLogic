# Code of Conduct

This framework was not written in the conventional sense. It was converged. Each concept began as a compressed prompt. That prompt was processed by multiple artificial systems. One generated. Another critiqued. Another restructured. Another exposed gaps. The outputs were synthesized, refined, and re-prompted. Iterations continued until uncertainty collapsed and the document appeared final. The author's role was not to write. The author's role was to define direction. To know what to ask. To recognize when the answer was complete.
