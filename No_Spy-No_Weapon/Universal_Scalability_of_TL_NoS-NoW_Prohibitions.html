Technical Friction

Latency overhead, provenance verification, and Epistemic Hold reduce competitiveness in microsecond workflows but are manageable in payments, infrastructure, and regulated compliance systems.

Political Pressure

National security, proprietary black-box interests, and defense procurement create strong incentives to fork or weaken the invariants.

Hardware Path

Near term: software emulation on binary hardware. Long term: selective ternary accelerators and ledger-specific silicon.

Oracle Problem

Immutable logs can preserve falsehoods if upstream sensors or APIs are compromised. Cross-verification and attestation are required to avoid deterministic false certainty.

Dual-Use Drift

Generic analytics can become surveillance through data and customer expansion. Restrict data categories, scale, and linkage paths.

Cyber/Weapons Leakage

Logistics, cyber defense, and intelligence analysis become non-compliant when tightly coupled to targeting or offensive action.
